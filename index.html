Firm Profile

Decades of practice.
A foundation you can trust.

Yemi Segun Ogundipe & Co (Chartered Accountants) is a firm of Chartered Accountants registered in Nigeria by the Company Registry at Abuja, Federal Capital Territory, authorised to carry on the business of statutory audit, tax, and management advisory services.

As a medium-scale indigenous firm, we bring the personal attention of a boutique practice with the rigour and methodology of a Big Four heritage. Our Managing Partner, Mr Yemi Ogundipe, is an alumnus of Coopers & Lybrand (now PricewaterhouseCoopers) with over 40 years of practice experience in accountancy, taxation, consulting, and IT.

We are presently pursuing strategic alliances with similar firms to broaden our service offering and build a sustainable practice for the future — while remaining deeply rooted in our Nigerian identity.

What We Do

Our Services

A comprehensive suite of professional services delivered with the rigour, discretion, and technology-backed methodology that modern Nigerian businesses demand.

Statutory Audit

Independent audit services for public and private sector clients, executed with meticulous documentation and professional scepticism — giving stakeholders the assurance they need.

Tax Compliance Audit

Comprehensive tax compliance reviews, advisory, and filing services designed to protect your business from exposure and optimise your tax position within Nigerian law.

Revenue Monitoring

Ongoing revenue assurance and monitoring engagements that identify leakages, verify completeness, and provide actionable intelligence to management and boards.

Forensic Investigation

Special investigations, including forensic audit, fraud examination, and litigation support — conducted with objectivity, confidentiality, and evidentiary rigour.

Management Consulting

Strategic and operational consulting services that help organisations build resilience, improve governance, and execute their business plans with greater confidence.

Staff Outsourcing & Recruitment

Head-hunting and outsourcing of high-skilled finance, IT, and accounting professionals — placing the right talent domestically and internationally, at short notice.

How We Work

A methodology built on
governance and trust.

The firm operates with standard questionnaires and full documentation of policies and procedures. Every engagement is governed by a formal confidentiality agreement and engagement letter, ensuring clarity and protecting your interests from day one.

Corporate governance is not an afterthought for us — it is embedded in how we run our own practice. Mr Yemi Ogundipe was part of the consortium that brought Professor Konner (a director of 20 Fortune 500 companies and PKF partner) to Nigeria in 2003 for a landmark Corporate Governance seminar at the Muson Centre.

Our own financials are independently audited year on year by a reputable firm — because we hold ourselves to the same standards we set for our clients.
